Contest Rules and Terms and Conditions

The contest will run from 8 November to 12 November , spanning 5 days.

Eligibility:

  • The contest is open to individuals of all ages and backgrounds.
    • Submission Requirements:
  • Each participant may submit only one selfie entry.
  • Selfies must be original and owned by the participant.
    • Voting Process:
  • Voting is open to the public and will take place on 8 November to 12 November.
  • Participants are encouraged to promote their entries to gather votes.
    • Determining the Winners:
  • The top five entries with the highest number of votes at the end of the contest will be declared the winners.
  • In case of a tie, the winners will be decided by organizer.
  • Winners will be announced on 13 November.
    • Prizes:
  • The top five winners will each get free professional photoshoot. The best photoshoots among the winners will receive an additional surprise gift!
    • Notification:
  • Winners will be notified via email or the contact information provided during submission.
  • If a winner cannot be reached within 7 days after notification, their prize may be forfeited.
    • Rights and Permissions:
  • By submitting an entry, participants grant the contest organizer the right to use and showcase their entry in promotional materials related to the contest.
    • Code of Conduct:
  • Inappropriate or offensive entries will be disqualified, and the participant may be banned from future contests.

Any attempts to manipulate votes or engage in fraudulent activities will result in disqualification.

Disclaimer:

The contest organizer reserves the right to modify or cancel the contest at their discretion if circumstances require it.
